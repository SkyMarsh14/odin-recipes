Tex-Mex Burger

This is the authentic tex-mex burger. It only takes 25min to make one! try it for yourself!

Here's all what you need to prepare!

  • 1/2 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon Cajun seasoning
  • 4 slices tomato
  • 4 leaves lettuce
  • 4 hamburger buns, split
  • 4 slices pepperjack cheese

It only takes 4 steps!

  1. Preheat grill for medium-high heat. In a samll bowl, mix together the mayonnaise and 1 teaspoon of Cajun seasoning. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl, mix together the ground sirloin, jalapeno pepper, onion, garlic, 1 tablespoon Cajun seasoning, and Worcestershire sauce using your hands. Divide into 4 balls, and flatten into patties.

  3. Lightly oil the grilling surface, and place the pattties on the grill. Cook for about 5 mins per side, or until well done.

    During the last 2 minuts, lay a slice of chesse on top of each patty. Spread the seasoned mayonnaise onto the insides of hte buns.

    Put burges in the buns, and top with lettuce and tomato to serve.
